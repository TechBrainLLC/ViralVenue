Viral Venue is a founder-led platform created with the goal of transforming rental event venue marketing. Our mission is simple: to connect event venue owners with social media influencers for the purpose of creating mutually beneficial relationships. Recognizing the many challenges that event venue owners face when marketing their venue, we aim to simplify the process, streamlining marketing whilst ensuring cost-effectiveness.

At the heart of our strategy is the use of small-scale social media influencers. Many marketing campaigns focus on large-scale influencers with world-wide influence, and while this approach serves the marketing of more mobile products remarkably, we believe the marketing of event venues requires a modified approach. By cultivating a network of small-scale, local influencers we aim to provide event venue owners with cheaper access to more authentic communities.

In our offered services, we aim to eliminate the multitude of barriers to entry present in the field of digital marketing, with our suite of tools being tailored to the needs of both venue owners and social media influencers. With AI tools capable of tasks ranging from creating optimal influencer-venue pairs to creating images of influencers in event venues with a couple base photos, we aim to ensure a better return on investment in comparison to traditional marketing methods.
